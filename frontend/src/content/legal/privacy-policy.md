# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

Make My Mock ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you
have.

## Information we collect

- **Account details** — your name, email address, and the exam/year you are
  preparing for, provided when you sign up or complete your profile.
- **Learning activity** — the tests you generate, questions you attempt, your
  answers, scores, streaks, and the analytics derived from them.
- **Technical data** — device and browser information, and data stored locally
  on your device (such as your login session and theme preference).

## How we use your information

- To provide the service — generating personalised mock tests, grading them,
  and showing your analytics.
- To power the recommender that weights questions toward your weakest topics.
- To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the platform.
- To contact you about your account, important updates, or support requests.

## Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. We share it only with:

- Service providers who help us operate the platform (for example, hosting and
  email delivery), bound by confidentiality obligations.
- Authorities, where required by law or to protect our rights and users.

## Data retention & security

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. We use reasonable
technical and organisational measures to protect it, though no method of
transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at
any time by emailing us. We will respond within a reasonable period.

## Children

Make My Mock is intended for students preparing for competitive exams. If you
are below the age of digital consent in your country, please use the platform
with the involvement of a parent or guardian.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by the "Last updated" date above.
