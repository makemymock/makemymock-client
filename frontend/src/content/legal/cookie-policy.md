# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

This policy explains how Make My Mock uses cookies and similar local-storage
technologies in your browser.

## What these technologies are

Cookies and browser storage are small pieces of data saved on your device that
let a site remember you between visits and page loads.

## What we use

- **Essential** — to keep you signed in (your session tokens) and to run core
  features. The platform stores these in your browser's local storage; without
  them you could not stay logged in.
- **Preferences** — to remember choices such as your light/dark theme.
- **Analytics** — to understand how the platform is used so we can improve it.
  These are aggregated and not used to identify you personally.

## Managing them

You can clear or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings.
Note that disabling essential storage will stop you from staying logged in and
may break parts of the platform.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. The "Last updated" date
above reflects the current version.
