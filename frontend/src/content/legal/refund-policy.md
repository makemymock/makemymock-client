# Refund Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

We want you to be happy with Make My Mock. This policy explains when refunds
apply.

## Free features

Much of Make My Mock is free to use. No payment means nothing to refund.

## Paid plans

If you purchase a paid subscription or plan:

- You may request a refund within **7 days** of purchase, provided the plan has
  not been substantially used (for example, a large share of premium tests or
  contests already consumed).
- Refunds are issued to your original payment method.

## Non-refundable cases

- Requests made after the 7-day window.
- Plans that have been substantially used.
- Charges for one-off contest entries or events that have already taken place.

## How to request a refund

Email **make.my.mock@gmail.com** from your registered email address with your
account details and the reason for the request. We typically respond within a
few business days, and approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days
depending on your bank or payment provider.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above
reflects the current version.

## Contact

Questions about a charge or refund? Email **make.my.mock@gmail.com**.
